Welcome to the study

This study is conducted by Elif Büşra Anlı as part of a Master's thesis at the Technical University of Munich (TUM).

If you have any questions regarding this study, you may contact the researcher at elif.anli@tum.de.

Thank you for participating in this study. In the following, you will be asked to read a short scenario and answer several questions.

The study is anonymous and your responses will be used for research purposes only. There are no right or wrong answers; we are interested in your personal judgment.

Participation is voluntary and you may stop at any time.

Please read all instructions carefully and answer the questions honestly.
