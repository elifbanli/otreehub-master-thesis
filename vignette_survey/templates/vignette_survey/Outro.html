Thank you for participating

The purpose of this study is to examine how people evaluate and use advice when it is provided by different types of sources.

During the study, participants were randomly assigned to one of two scenarios. In one scenario, the advice was presented as coming from an AI-based system, while in the other scenario, the same advice was presented as coming from a human tutor.

By comparing responses across these two conditions, we aim to better understand how the perceived source of advice influences trust, confidence, and decision-making.

Thank you very much for your time and valuable contribution to this research.
